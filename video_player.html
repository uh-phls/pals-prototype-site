Here we will post our program videos. But instead of having a five, six minute long video about our program and our research, I want to break things down a bit more. I want to have the main video be about our program, what makes it unique and special. From there, I want each of our faculty members to have a short video about their research. Each video will also include a bit about why our program is worth paying attention to. This way we make the content more easily digestible to our users. We also make it easier for us to update content when and if changes to our faculty take place.
